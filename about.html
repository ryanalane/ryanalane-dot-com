about

Hi there, I'm Ryan. I care about a lot of things, but it's safe to sum up that I write—code, journals, design sketches, and the occasional blog post about my work and travels.

Developing software feels like a calling to me if anything does, and it plays nicely with my other interests: music, design, fiction, foreign languages, cities—areas that help me connect with other humans and know what it means to be alive. These complements keep me from becoming a robot sooner than I have to.

I study Software Engineering at Arizona State University. I've been flirting with a bunch of possible secondary concentrations for my final two years there. I'm currently taking time off to intern for Prezi in Budapest, Hungary.

If you think we should talk, I'd love to meet. Conversing with good people is my favorite thing in the world.

Let's talk.
